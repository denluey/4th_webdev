Due to the effects of the prison industrial complex, multiple different groups have been negativly effected. Women find themselves treated unfairly and violated by the prisons they are kept in, with no hope of reform due to the greedy nature of companies who profit off of the prisons. And not only women, but minorities find themselves occupying 70 percent of the prison population

Examples of the prison industrial complex range far and wide, from private prisons incentivized to keep their jail cells full in order to keep raking in bail and money which creates the conditions that turn people to crime, to the almost slave labor that is forced upon prisoners
